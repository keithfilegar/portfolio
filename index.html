About Me

After being honorably discharged from the US Navy, I began working in recruiting at a quickly growing software company. In my time spent there I developed a strong interest in how software is made, and eventually I transitioned into a role as a QA Engineer. After some time writing automation tests, I finally realized that it was the right time to transition into software development.

My time spent in the Navy and as a QA engineer, has taught me how to collaborate well with diverse teams. I've worked as a member of large and small teams with both in-person and distributed members.

I am well versed in fullstack web development, Agile/SCRUM development methodologies, and test driven development. If you're looking to hire a new developer, please review my portfolio below!
